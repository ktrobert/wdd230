Life in Carlsbad

Carlsbad is a beautiful small city that has beautiful sandy beaches, lagoons, great shopping and world-class sporting events. It is close to LEGOLAND® theme park with rides. The Flower Fields of Carlsbad Ranch supports 50 acres of magnificent flowers every spring. Home to many world-renowned golf equipment manufacturers, Carlsbad also has the La Costa golf courses where famouse golf players come together all over the world.
