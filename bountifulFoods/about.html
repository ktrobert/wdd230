ABOUT US

Our Town

Carlsbad is a small town near San Diego, California, well known for Tamarack Surf Beach and South Carlsbad State Beach. Home to The Legoland California theme park includes rides, a water park and the SEA LIFE Aquarium. The Flower Fields is a seasonal attraction, with acres of flowers. The Leo Carrillo Ranch Historic Park is home to free-roaming peacocks. A wonderful place to visit!

Our Brand

Bountiful Foods has been providing fresh and organic poduce since 1987. We are proud of our brand because we only offer the freshest fruits, free of pesticides and naturally grown. There are plenty of options to choose from. You can also design your own fruit mix by choosing from a variety of fruits from our selection.

Our Product

Along with our produce, we also provide fresh juice. Our FRESH page has our selection of fruits that you can choose from to make your very own fruit drink. From Oranges, strawberries, rasberries, dragonfruits, mangos, or any vegetables. You name it, we mix it!

Our Farm

We grow our own produce. Our farm is close by so we can bring in fresh fruits and vegetables everyday. Come and see what we have in store for you.
