Discover what the Fork has to offer...

American Fork City has so much to offer. Timpanogos Cave and the Tibble Fork Reservoir are some of the most breathtaking sites to explore. The fall season is where it's at because of the extraordinary mountain views with the vibrant fall colors.

Meet your neighbors! There's quite a charm in small town living. There is a great diversity of people.

Did anyone say SHOPPING? There is a shopping center near you. Whether it's spending time at the mall, go to a department store, or getting your groceries, we have got all your needs.

Timpanogos Temple is the heart of American Fork City. Well known as the Lord's House, Timpanogos Temple is a breathtaking building that sits by the mountain.

Need transportation? A newly built FrontRunner train station is set to help those to get away from traffic and protect the environment.
