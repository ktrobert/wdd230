News Release

Great news! The American Fork Chamber City of Commerce’s Presidential, Elite, Premier, Trustee and Community Partner Level members are invited to participate in an exclusive quarterly roundtable meeting with the mayors and city staff of American Fork, Alpine, Cedar Hills, and Highland as well as with local legislators to discuss issues affecting businesses and to provide an essential business voice to city, county, state, and federal leaders.

The dates for the 2023 Executive Forum meetings are below. All meetings are held at Altabank in American Fork and go from 11:30 am to 1:00 pm. Lunch is provided.

  • Wednesday, February 22 (All 4 Mayors)
  • Tuesday, April 18 (State and Federal Legislators)
  • Tuesday, June 20 (American Fork Mayor)
  • Tuesday, August 15 (Governor's/Attorney General's Office)
  • Tuesday, October 17 (Lone Peak Mayors)
  • Tuesday, December 19 (County Commissioners)
  • Wednesday, February 22 (All 4 Mayors)
