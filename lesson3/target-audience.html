Target Audience

Our audience will consist of entrepreneurs, existing businesses, the community, and pro-business members. Entrepreneurs, whether they are starting up their potential businesses. Existing businesses that want growth. Community-minded people that are investing in the influence of the future of the community. Pro-business members that care about public support for or recommendation and its environment.

Scenarios

"How can I build a network to expand my business?" We offer members the opportunity to network with other companies in the community with events like luncheons, conventions, or community service projects.

"How can I market my business?" We provide members with advertising opportunities on our websites and we can publish newsletters, maintain social media pages, and run publications.

"Do you do any fundraisers for members?" We offer fundraisers for member businesses with local events and help partnership for members.

"Will you provide new business opportunities?" We provide business referrals, insights into new local businesses, and networking opportunities with industry leaders.

"How old do you have to be to join?" You have to be 18 years and older and have a business license.
