Overview

Purpose

The purpose for this website is to help local American Fork businesses to put their minds together to help the growth of the community, whether it be economic, social, individual, or to promote one's business. We are here to gather information about the local business to build interest and education for what's available in our community.

Audience

We want to reach everyone in the community of American Fork. We want to reach out to business owners and help them if they are in need of employees or just want to promote their businesses. We also want to reach out to those in search of employment opportunities with our local businesses.
